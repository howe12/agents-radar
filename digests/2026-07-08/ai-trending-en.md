## 3. Trend Signal Analysis

The dominant signal across today's trending list is the **emergence of a layered "Agent OS" stack**. At the substrate level, **TencentCloud/CubeSandbox** (+664) addresses safe execution for untrusted agent code, while **iOfficeAI/OfficeCLI** (+893) reframes legacy desktop software as agent primitives. Above them, **agent-skills** (+1,317) and **dotnet/skills** (+64) provide reusable domain knowledge, and **bradautomates/claude-video** (+965) injects new sensory modalities. This pattern — sandbox + skills + tool surface + modalities — closely mirrors how Linux tooling ossified around the kernel in the early 2000s.

A second clear signal is the **local-AI renaissance**. **Meetily** (+1,777), **pocket-tts** (+531), **CodexBar** (+376), and the persistent popularity of **Ollama** (175k⭐) reveal a developer base that is no longer satisfied with cloud-only stacks. Mac/Windows users now expect Whisper-class transcription, TTS, and LLM inference running 100% locally — a strong privacy/UX differentiator.

The **"commercial-AI transparency"** subculture also resurged via the system-prompts leak repo (+1,691), with mentions of Claude Opus 4.8, GPT 5.5, Gemini 3.5, and Grok — indicating these are the bleeding-edge frontier models devs want to benchmark and reverse-engineer in July 2026. **Picovoice/picollm** and **headroom** point to growing interest in **token-efficient inference**, while **[RSS 2026] RISE** and **CLP_VLA** demonstrate that Vision-Language-Action models have become the canonical research vehicle for generalist robotics.
